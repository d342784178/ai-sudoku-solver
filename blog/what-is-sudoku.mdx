# Sudoku: The Brain-Boosting Number Game

## Introduction

[Sudoku](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sudoku) is a popular number puzzle game that is played globally. This challenging yet captivating game is not only a great way to pass time, but it's also beneficial for enhancing cognitive skills, memory, and concentration. If you haven't discovered the joy and benefits of Sudoku yet, this article is just for you.

## What is Sudoku?

Sudoku is a brain-challenging puzzle that requires players to fill a 9x9 grid with numbers so that each column, each row, and each of the nine 3x3 subgrids that compose the grid contains all of the digits from 1 to 9. The puzzle starts with a partially completed grid and the player has to fill in the rest of the grid with the right numbers.

```
-------------------------
| 5 3 . | . . 7 | . . . |
| 6 . . | 1 9 5 | . . . |
| . 9 8 | . . . | . 6 . |
-------------------------
| 8 . . | . 6 . | . . 3 |
| 4 . . | 8 . 3 | . . 1 |
| 7 . . | . 2 . | . . 6 |
-------------------------
| . 6 . | . . . | 2 8 . |
| . . . | 4 1 9 | . . 5 |
| . . . | . . 8 | . 7 9 |
-------------------------
```
## Origin of Sudoku

Despite its Japanese name, the Sudoku puzzle has its origins from the Swiss mathematician [Leonhard Euler](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Leonhard_Euler) in the 18th century. However, it was not until the late 20th century that Sudoku became a widespread game in Japan through magazines. Since then, it has gained global popularity and is now found in many newspapers, websites, and [mobile apps](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=uk.co.aifactory.sudokufree&hl=en&gl=US) worldwide.

## Benefits of Playing Sudoku

Playing Sudoku offers numerous mental benefits, some of which include:

1. **Improves Memory:** Sudoku boosts your memory and recall by making you think and remember numbers.
2. **Enhances Logical Thinking:** By solving Sudoku puzzles, you can improve your logical and critical thinking skills as they require a strategic approach to problem-solving.
3. **Reduces Anxiety and Stress:** Sudoku is a calming activity that can help reduce stress and anxiety by focusing your mind on the puzzle rather than on your worries.
4. **Prevents Cognitive Decline:** Regularly engaging in puzzles like Sudoku can help keep your brain sharp and slow cognitive decline associated with aging, as reported by the [New York Times](https://www.nytimes.com/2012/04/22/magazine/can-you-boost-your-brain-power.html).
